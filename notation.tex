\cleardoublepage\chapter*{Definitions}\label{chap:definitions}\addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Definitions}
\begin{itemize}
	\item \textbf{Queue} -- a line of people, vehicles or other objects. The first are dealt with first, so it is said to have a first-in first-out order, \emph{FIFO}.\index{queue}\index{FIFO}
	\item \textbf{Up-time} -- arrival of a vehicle.\index{up-time}
	\item \textbf{Down-time} -- departure of a vehicle.\index{down-time}
	\item \textbf{On-time} -- time between arrival and departure of a vehicle.\index{on-time}
\end{itemize}

In this thesis the magnetic field strength refers to the magnetic field strength \emph{relative} to the earth magnetic field strength unless otherwise stated. The measured quantity is the disturbance of the earth magnetic field so this notation is applicable.
